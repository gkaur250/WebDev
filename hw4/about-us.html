Our Mission

At Toddler Interns Inc., our mission is to provide toddlers with early exposure to various career fields, allowing them to gain valuable work experience from a young age. Through tasks such as building toys, coloring books, and handling paperwork, toddlers develop essential skills and lay the foundation for future success in the workforce. We are committed to offering opportunities for growth and learning, ensuring that each toddler intern receives the support they need to thrive.
